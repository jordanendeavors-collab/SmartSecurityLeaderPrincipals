Core Philosophy: Bridging vs. Bonding

Bridging = Connecting with people DIFFERENT from you (cross-cultural, cross-departmental, cross-generational)

Bonding = Connecting with people SIMILAR to you (shared backgrounds, experiences, communities)

True inclusive leaders excel at BOTH ‚Äî and know when to use each approach.
